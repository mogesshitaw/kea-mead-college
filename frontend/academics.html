Academic Programs

Doctor of Medicine (MD)

Our flagship program provides comprehensive medical education with clinical training.

  • 4-year program
  • Clinical rotations
  • Research opportunities

The MD program at Kea Med College is designed to train physicians who are not only clinically competent but also compassionate, ethical, and committed to lifelong learning.

Bachelor of Nursing (BSN)

Comprehensive nursing education with hands-on clinical experience.

  • 3-year program
  • Clinical practice
  • Specialization options

Our BSN program prepares students for successful careers in nursing through a combination of theoretical knowledge and extensive clinical practice in various healthcare settings.

Medical Laboratory Science

Training in laboratory diagnostics, research methods, and medical technology.

  • 3-year program
  • Lab techniques
  • Research focus

This program equips students with the skills needed to work in clinical laboratories, conducting tests that aid in diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases.
