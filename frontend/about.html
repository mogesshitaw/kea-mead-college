About Kea Med College

Pioneering Medical Education for Over Three Decades

Our History

Founded in 1985, Kea Med College has been at the forefront of medical education and healthcare innovation for over 35 years. Our institution was established with the vision of creating a world-class medical education center that would serve not only our local community but also contribute to global healthcare advancements.

Over the decades, we have grown from a small medical school to a comprehensive medical college with multiple specialized departments, research centers, and teaching hospitals. Our alumni network spans across the globe, with graduates making significant contributions to healthcare in over 50 countries.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in medical education, research, and healthcare delivery, producing compassionate and competent healthcare professionals who will lead innovation in medicine and improve human health worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide outstanding medical education through innovative teaching, cutting-edge research, and exceptional clinical experiences. We are committed to developing skilled, ethical, and compassionate physicians and healthcare leaders who will serve diverse communities with excellence and integrity.
