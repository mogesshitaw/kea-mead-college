Student Services

Comprehensive Support for Academic and Personal Success

Student Support Services

Academic Advising

Personalized guidance on course selection, academic planning, and career pathways from experienced faculty advisors.

Our academic advisors help students navigate their medical education journey, providing support for academic challenges and planning for future specialization.

Counseling Services

Confidential mental health support and wellness resources to help students manage stress and maintain wellbeing.

Our licensed counselors provide individual and group sessions, crisis intervention, and workshops on stress management, resilience, and work-life balance.

Career Services

Comprehensive career planning, residency application support, and networking opportunities with healthcare organizations.

We offer CV and personal statement reviews, mock interviews, career fairs, and connections with our extensive alumni network in various medical specialties.

Campus Facilities

Medical Library

Our state-of-the-art medical library provides access to extensive collections of medical literature, journals, and online databases.

  • 24/7 access for students
  • Quiet study areas and group collaboration spaces
  • Research assistance from medical librarians
  • Access to online medical databases and e-journals

IT Support

Comprehensive technology services to support academic and research needs.

  • 24/7 technical support
  • Campus-wide WiFi
  • Computer labs with specialized medical software
  • Learning management system support

Cafeteria & Dining

Multiple dining options offering healthy and diverse meal choices.

  • Main cafeteria with varied menu options
  • Starbucks coffee shop
  • Healthy snack bars throughout campus
  • Special dietary accommodations available

Recreation Center

Fitness and wellness facilities to support student health and wellbeing.

  • Fully equipped gym
  • Indoor swimming pool
  • Basketball and volleyball courts
  • Yoga and meditation classes

Student Housing

On-Campus Residence Halls

Safe, comfortable, and convenient housing options located within walking distance of all academic buildings.

  • Single and double occupancy rooms
  • Furnished rooms with study areas
  • Shared kitchen and lounge facilities
  • 24/7 security and residential advisors
  • High-speed internet included

Off-Campus Housing Assistance

Resources and support for students seeking housing in the surrounding community.

  • Database of approved rental properties
  • Roommate matching service
  • Rental agreement review
  • Commuting resources and parking information

Student Health Services

The Kea Med College Health Center provides comprehensive medical care for all enrolled students. Our services include:

  • Primary medical care
  • Immunizations and health screenings
  • Women's health services
  • Mental health counseling
  • Laboratory services
  • Pharmacy services
  • Health education and wellness programs
  • Emergency care coordination

Hours: Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Saturday 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Contact: (555) 123-4567 | healthcenter@keamedcollege.edu

Student Health Insurance

All students are required to have health insurance coverage. We offer a comprehensive student health insurance plan.

Disability Support Services

Kea Med College is committed to providing equal access to educational opportunities for students with disabilities. Our Disability Support Services office works with students to arrange appropriate accommodations.

  • Academic accommodations
  • Assistive technology resources
  • Accessibility coordination
  • Exam accommodations

Students needing accommodations should contact the Disability Support Services office at the beginning of each semester.
