Research

Advancing Medical Knowledge Through Innovation

Our Research Mission

At Kea Med College, research is integral to our mission of advancing medical knowledge and improving patient care. Our faculty and students engage in cutting-edge research across multiple disciplines, from basic science to clinical applications.

We foster a collaborative research environment that encourages innovation and translational research, with the goal of bringing scientific discoveries from the laboratory to the bedside.

Featured Research Projects

This project aims to identify novel genomic markers that can detect cancer at its earliest stages, improving treatment outcomes.

Principal Investigator: Dr. Emily Watson

Funding: National Institutes of Health

Student Research Opportunities

Summer Research Program

An intensive 8-week program where students work closely with faculty mentors on ongoing research projects.

  • Open to all medical students
  • Stipend provided
  • Presentation opportunity at annual research symposium

Year-Long Research Fellowships

For students interested in deeper research immersion, these fellowships allow participation in long-term projects.

  • Available for 2nd and 3rd year students
  • Can fulfill research elective requirements
  • Potential for co-authorship on publications
