Admission Requirements

General Requirements

  • Completion of secondary education with strong science background
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale (or equivalent)
  • Competitive scores on entrance examination (KMCAT)
  • Proficiency in English (TOEFL/IELTS for international students)
  • Letters of recommendation (2 required)
  • Personal statement and interview
